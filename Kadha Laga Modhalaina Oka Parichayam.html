Introduction

Ee kadhalo hero ledu… heroine ledu.

Idi manchi ani, adi chedhu ani judge cheyyadaniki kuda kaadhu. Prathi aarambham lo oka thipi gurtu untundi, Prathi parichayam lo konchem chedhu kuda kalisuntundi. Jeevitham ala nadusthune untundi… Manam ardham chesina, chesakapoyina. Kaani chivariki Matti lo kalisaka migiledhi oka gnyapakam maatrame. Memories manchi aina, chedhu aina… Kaalam tho vati sambandham undadhu. Avi akkadey nilichipothayi. Kaani nijam enti ante Chivari kshanam varaku, Okarikosam eduruchuse nirishna manishi vadilipettadu.

“Marpu” ane peru tho… Manalo unna nijamaina manam ni maname champesthunnaam. Endhuku? “Neeku kosam brathuku… society kosam kaadhu” ani cheppe ade society,

Malli “status undali, image maintain cheyyali” ani pressure peduthundi. Nee tappu ani cheppalem…

Society tappu ani simple ga blame cheyyalem. Mari inka aavaru?

Allaga charitra lo leni socity lo unna mistakes ni correct chedam ani try chesi last ki ade matilo kalisina....

Oka chinna kadha ..............................

“Chinna kadha” ante thakkuva rojulu garijina kadha ani kadhu .........

society ki enta chepina vinpinchaledu ani “oka chinna kadha “ ani cheppanu

Ee kadha ippudu jarigindhi kaadhu. Ilanti kathalu eppatiki jarugutune untayi. Kaani prathi sari okate tag pettadam

#societydown, #systemfail ani simplify chesthe,Aa kadha lo unna nijamaina value taggipothundi.

Endhukante…

Prathi kadha blame kosam kaadhu. Konni kathalu ardham kosam. Konni reflection kosam.

Society ni blame cheyyadam easy… Kaani manam, mana bhayam, mana compromises

Ivvi kuda aa kathalo part avuthayi.

Anduke…
