To seek the treasure is to renounce the world—not to escape it, but to transcend it.” In an age of kingdoms and crowns, where valor is measured by conquest and legacy is weighed in gold, one prince dares to ask the question no one else will: What lies beyond power? This is the story of Dhruvendra, heir to the golden throne of Aranyapura, who chooses the flute’s haunting call over the fanfare of coronation. Haunted by dreams of a melody that comes not from this world but from deep within his soul, he sets forth on a path no map can truly chart—a path of surrender, transformation, and awakening. Along the way, he must part with everything he once called his: – a beloved friend turned rival, – a lover whose eyes hold both devotion and doubt, – and the crown he was born to wear but not meant to bear

Each chapter is a step through fire, silence, loss, and inner reckoning—Until he faces the final truth: The greatest treasure is not hidden in gold or guarded in temples.It is the still point within the soul, waiting to be remembered. This book is not just a tale—it is an offering to every reader who has ever questioned the world’s noise and longed for a deeper music. Whether you are a seeker of truth, a lover of myth, or someone standing on the edge of change

May this story mirror your own journey home. Welcome to the silence. Welcome to Dhruvendra – the treasure seeker
