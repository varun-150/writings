Chapter 01

Anthima Veedkolu

Dwapara Yuga antham. Krishna Vrindavan ni vadili velladam. Oka chinna Gopika hrudayam virigipovadam.

Scroll to begin

Dwapara Yuga antham daggaraku vachindi. Vrindavan lo aa rojulu maripothunnayi. Krishna Mathura ki velladaniki siddham ayyadu. Gramam motham baadha lo munigipoyindi. Gopikalu andariki telusu... Krishna vellipothe malli aa rojulu tirigi ravu ani.

Aakasham kuda aa roju nisshabdanga undi. Yamuna teeram daggara oka chinna Gopika nilchoni undi. Andaru Krishna ni Bhagavan ani pilustharu. Kaani aame matram eppudu alaa pilavaledu.

“
Aame hrudayam lo Krishna ante devudu kaadu. Aame jeevitham. Aame praanam. Aame prema.
”

Krishna ratham munduku kadaladam modalayindi. Andaru edusthunaru. Kaani aa ammayi maatram maatladalekapoyindi. Aame kallalo neeru nilichipoyindi.

Gopika

"Krishna..."

Krishna venakki tirigi choosadu. Chinna navvu navvadu. Aa navvu aame jeevitham lo chivari velugu ani aameku teliyadu. Ratham dooram avutoo undi. Krishna roopam chinnaga marutoo undi. Aame gunde lo edo virigipoyindi.

Gopika

"Enduku vellipothunnavu Krishna?"

Samadhanam ledu.

Kevalam dooram avutunna ratham matrame kanipinchindi. Aa roju Vrindavan nundi Krishna vellaledu. Oka hrudayam nundi prema kuda vellipoyindi.

Chapter 02

Oka Janma Mugisindi

Viraha vedana lo aa Gopika pranalu vidavadam.

Scroll to begin

Krishna vellina taruvata rojulu gadusthunnayi. Kaani aa Gopika jeevitham matram akkade aagipoyindi. Andaru munduku saagipoyaru. Kaani aame prathi roju Yamuna teeram ki velli Krishna kosam eduruchusedi.

Gopika

"Repu vastadu..."

Aa repu eppudu raledu. Oka saayantram. Aame okkate koorchoni undi. Aakasham orange rangulo merusthundi. Gaali mellaga veesthundi. Dooram nundi flute laanti shabdam vinipinchindi. Aame navvindi.

Gopika

"Krishna vachava?"

Kaani akkada evaru leru. Aame kallalo neeru cherindi.

Gopika

"Marichipoyava nannu?"

Samadhanam ledu.

Aa raatri aame Krishna peru japistune nidrapoyindi. Malli levaledu.

03
Chapter 03

Maro Prayanam

Aame aatma punarjanma kosam prayanam modalupettadam.

Scroll to begin

Shariram bhoomi meeda migilipoyindi. Kaani prema migilindi. Aatma migilindi.

Aa aatma kaalam ane nadi lo prayaanam modalupettindi. Yugalu marayi. Rajyalu puttayi. Rajyalu nashinchipoyayi. Kaani aa prema maatram maaraledu.

“
Oka vaagdanam laa. Oka asampoorna kathala.
”

Aa aatma malli puttadaniki siddham ayyindi.

Krishna ni malli vetakadaniki.

Chapter 04

Abhinaya Jananam

Dance parampara kutumbam lo Abhinaya puttadam.

Scroll to begin

Samvatsaralu gadichayi. Oka chinna rajyam. Krishna bhakti ki prasiddhi pondina bhoomi. Akkada dance parampara unna kutumbam lo oka paapa puttindi. Intlo andaroo santoshamga unnaru. Paapa puttina ventane navvindi.

Amma

"Mana intiki Lakshmi vachindi."

Nanna

"Ee ammayi goppaga perugutundi."

Aameku peru pettaru. “Abhinaya”. Aame peru pettina kshanam nundi intlo unna deepam balanga veliginattu anipinchindi.

Evariki teliyadu.

Kaani oka puratana prema malli bhoomi meeda adugu pettindi.

Chapter 05

Ajnatha Gnapakalu

Chinnappati nundi Krishna gurinchi vintunna kalalu.

Scroll to begin

Abhinaya chinnaga periguthundi. Migatha pillalu bommalatho aadukuntaru. Kaani Abhinaya ki vere ishtalu. Aame eppudu Krishna bomma daggare untundi.

Amma

"Abhi, enduku eppudu Krishna daggare untavu?"

Abhinaya

"Naku ayana ante chala ishtam."

Amma

"Bhagavan kabatti kada?"

Abhinaya

"Ledu..."

Amma

"Marenduku?"

Abhinaya

"Naku teliyadu... kaani ayana dooram vellipoyina feeling vastundi."

Aa maataki amma navvindi. Kaani enduko aame gunde lo oka chinna bayam puttindi. Aa raatri Abhinaya oka kala chusindi.

“
Yamuna teeram.... Venugaanam.... Dooram vellipothunna Krishna....
”

Kallu terichindi. Kallalo neeru undi. Tanaki ardham kaaledu.

Kaani oka vishayam maatram telusu. Ekkado... Eppudo... Tanu Krishna ni kolpoyindi.
